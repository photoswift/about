• Data Collection Statement

We take your privacy very seriously.

  1. KeySnip does not actively collect, store, or upload any text content you input or save via the keyboard (including chat content, input content, etc.).
  2. All your custom data (such as texts, snippets, favorites, etc.) is stored exclusively on your local device.
  3. Biometric Data: This app offers a privacy lock feature based on Face ID / Touch ID. Authentication is completed locally by the iOS system. We do not collect, read, store, or upload any biometric data (including facial data, fingerprint data, etc.).

• Data Control

You may delete local data within the app at any time, or remove all content by uninstalling the app. We cannot access or recover any data you have deleted.

• Keyboard Extension Privacy Statement

KeySnip provides a custom keyboard extension. According to iOS system mechanisms, users may choose whether to enable "Allow Full Access".

  • Without Full Access: Core keyboard features will be limited. The keyboard cannot read or update your saved custom content, favorites, or recent usage data.
  • With Full Access: Used to read and update your custom content, favorites, and recent usage data saved in KeySnip. We do not collect or upload any content you type via the keyboard.

• Third-Party SDKs

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect user data. There is no third-party data tracking or advertising behavior.

• Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy in the future, we will notify you within the app or through other appropriate means.

• Permissions and Use Cases

The following explains the permissions this app may request. We will only request permissions necessary for specific features. If you do not agree to grant a permission, tap "Don't Allow". You may also revoke previously granted permissions in your device settings at any time. Denying or revoking a permission will only affect your use of the related feature.

  • Network Access: Used to load in-app web content (such as Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages), process subscription purchase requests, and check for app version updates.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: Used for local authentication of the category privacy lock. Authentication is performed entirely on-device. We do not obtain any biometric data.
  • Clipboard Access: Used to read clipboard content for features such as paste import and quick access to copied content. We do not collect or upload any data from your clipboard.
